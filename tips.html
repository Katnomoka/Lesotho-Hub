Business Tips for Farmers

1. Keep Records

Always record your sales, expenses, and profits. This helps you track progress and manage your farm like a business.

2. Diversify Your Products

Don’t rely on one crop or animal. Diversification reduces risk and ensures stable income throughout the year.

3. Use Social Media

Promote your farm online to reach more customers. Simple Facebook or WhatsApp ads can bring big sales opportunities.

4. Maintain Quality

High-quality produce builds customer trust. Always ensure fresh, clean, and well-packaged products.

5. Collaborate with Other Farmers

Work together to sell in bulk, reduce costs, and reach larger markets like supermarkets and schools.
